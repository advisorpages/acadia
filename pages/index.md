## How to Use This Guide

Each section builds upon the previous one, creating a natural flow that guides prospects through understanding their financial situation and seeing the value you provide. The script includes real dialogue examples and responses to help you prepare for actual conversations.

## Key Features

- **Real conversation examples** with actual prospect responses
- **Step-by-step guidance** through the entire presentation process
- **Multiple scenarios** for different types of prospects (clients, partners, referrers)
- **Practical language** you can use immediately
- **Natural transitions** between topics

Navigate through each section in order, or jump to specific scenarios that match your current needs.
