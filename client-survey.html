CLIENT SURVEY

I. INTRODUCTION
  • Why did you want to meet with me today?
  • How did you hear about us?

II. ABOUT YOU: Current Situation

Family & Home

  • Tell me a little about your family.
  • Are you married or single?
  • Do you have children?
  • Who relies on you for money?

Work & Money

  • What do you do for work?
  • How long have you been doing that?
  • What do you like best about your job?
  • If you could change one thing, what would it be?
  • If you could pick your yearly income, what would it be?

III. YOUR FUTURE: Dreams & Goals

Life Goals

  • If time and money were not an issue, what would your life look like?
  • What are your most important goals right now?
  • Do you have dreams you have put on hold?
  • Describe your perfect lifestyle.

Retirement

  • At what age do you want to stop working?
  • Are you on track to retire at that age?

IV. REALITY CHECK: Where You Are Now

Current Plan

  • Do you have someone helping you plan right now?
  • How confident do you feel about your money?
  • Is there anything you are worried about?

The Obstacles

  • Will your current job allow you to reach your goals?
  • What is stopping you from getting there?

V. NEXT STEPS

  • Would you be open to looking at better options?
  • What do you need the most help with right now?
