# Client Discovery Questionnaire
### *Building Meaningful Conversations*

**Purpose**: This guide helps us have a natural conversation to better understand what's important to you and your family. Use these questions as conversation starters, not a checklist - focus on listening and letting the discussion flow naturally.

---

## Section 1: Professional Background
*(Ice Breaker - Learn about their work and what drives them)*

**Conversation Starters:**
- What do you do for work?
- What's the most rewarding part of what you do?
- If you had a magic wand, what would you change about your work situation?
- What originally drew you to your current career path?

## Agent Guidelines

### How to Use This Questionnaire:
1. **Natural Flow**: Use questions as conversation starters, not an interrogation checklist
2. **Listen First**: Focus on what they say vs. filling out forms
3. **Take Brief Notes**: Jot key insights during natural conversation pauses
4. **Stay Conversational**: Maintain the "getting to know you" tone throughout
5. **Adapt as Needed**: Skip questions that don't fit the conversation flow

### Integration with Financial House:
- **Foundation Level**: Use personal insights to understand what needs protection
- **Basics Level**: Identify family priorities for emergency planning and legal documents
- **Structure Level**: Connect dreams to concrete financial planning needs
- **Roof Level**: Help them see how planning supports their vision of freedom

### Three Path Preparation:
- **Client Services**: Personal insights guide which Financial House sections to emphasize
- **Team Recruitment**: Career satisfaction questions identify potential business opportunity
- **Referrals**: Understanding their values helps identify who they might want to help

### Key Success Indicators:
✓ Prospect feels heard and understood
✓ Natural conversation flows beyond surface level
✓ You have clear insights about their family, values, and dreams
✓ Transition to Financial House feels logical and personalized
✓ Next steps conversation feels natural, not forced
